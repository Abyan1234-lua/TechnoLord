# Measurement Framework

## Objective

Enable reliable budget and scaling decisions using full-funnel performance and business impact metrics.

## Measurement Layers

| Layer | Purpose | Metrics |
|---|---|---|
| Traffic | Diagnose acquisition efficiency | CTR, CPC, CPM, impression share |
| Conversion | Validate on-site and lead performance | CVR, CPA, cost per qualified lead |
| Revenue | Evaluate business contribution | ROAS, contribution margin, payback period |
| Growth | Track long-term impact | LTV, repeat rate, brand search lift |

## Required Tracking Setup

- Google Tag Manager implemented and validated.
- Enhanced Conversions enabled.
- Offline conversion import for lead quality and closed revenue.
- Platform pixels configured for retargeting and audience sync.

## Reporting Cadence

- **Daily:** spend pacing, delivery anomalies, broken tracking alerts.
- **Weekly:** channel performance, creative tests, negative keyword updates.
- **Monthly:** contribution margin by channel, cohort quality, scale decisions.

## Decision Rules

- Scale when efficiency is stable and margin-positive over the agreed data window.
- Hold when results are mixed and sample size is insufficient.
- Cut or rework when spend is statistically meaningful with persistent underperformance.
