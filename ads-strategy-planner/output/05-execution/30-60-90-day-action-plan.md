# 30-60-90 Day Action Plan

## Days 1-30: Foundation and Launch

### Priorities
- Finalize campaign architecture and naming conventions.
- Launch Search + Shopping (Google) and TOFU/MOFU baseline (LinkedIn).
- Deploy measurement infrastructure (GTM, Enhanced Conversions, offline events).
- Build first creative test matrix from existing winners and case-study learnings.

### Deliverables
- Active baseline campaigns
- Tracking QA sign-off
- Initial negative keyword framework
- Weekly reporting dashboard v1

## Days 31-60: Optimization and Qualification

### Priorities
- Execute weekly search query mining and negative updates.
- Run structured creative reskin tests using 70/20/10 allocation.
- Optimize funnel friction based on quality vs volume signals.
- Tighten LinkedIn qualification through Lead Gen Forms and CRM routing.

### Deliverables
- Creative leaderboard with scale decisions
- Funnel conversion audit with test outcomes
- Lead quality scorecard by channel

## Days 61-90: Scale and Expansion

### Priorities
- Add Performance Max once stability threshold is met.
- Add Demand Gen for top-funnel expansion where justified.
- Scale winning LinkedIn audience-creative combinations.
- Reallocate budgets based on contribution margin and qualified pipeline impact.

### Deliverables
- Scaled campaign set with governance guardrails
- Monthly business-impact review (LTV, margin, payback)
- Q2/Q3 scaling recommendations

## Governance Rhythm

- **Weekly growth meeting:** optimization decisions and blockers.
- **Bi-weekly creative review:** hooks, proof assets, and fatigue management.
- **Monthly executive review:** budget shifts, scale decisions, and forecast updates.
