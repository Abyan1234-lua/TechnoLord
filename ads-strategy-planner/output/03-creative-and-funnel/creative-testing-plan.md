# Creative Testing Plan

## Objective

Increase paid efficiency by scaling proven creatives and systematically testing high-impact variants.

## Testing Model (70/20/10)

| Allocation | Purpose |
|---|---|
| 70% | Scale current winning creatives |
| 20% | Test adjacent variations of winners |
| 10% | Explore new creative angles and formats |

## Creative Development Workflow

1. Collect top organic and paid performers by saves, follows, CTR, CVR, and CPA.
2. Select top winners for reskinning.
3. Produce variants across:
   - Hook (first 3 seconds)
   - Headline/subheadline
   - Visual treatment (filters, layout)
   - CTA framing
4. Launch in controlled test cells with clear pass/fail thresholds.
5. Promote winners to scale tier and archive underperformers.

## Pass/Fail Criteria (Example)

- Pass to scale when:
  - CTR >= baseline + 15%
  - CVR >= baseline + 10%
  - CPA <= baseline - 10%
- Kill or rework when:
  - Spend reaches threshold without conversion efficiency.

## Reporting Template

Track weekly by creative ID:
- Spend
- Impressions
- CTR
- CVR
- CPA/CAC
- Qualified lead rate
- Notes (hook, proof type, audience fit)
