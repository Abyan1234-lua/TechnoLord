# Executive Summary

## Objective

Build a scalable paid acquisition system that combines:
- Organic content signal harvesting
- AI-native Google Ads execution
- High-intent funnel optimization
- Profit-first measurement

## Strategic Thesis

The highest-performing ad system is built on two reinforcing loops:
1. **Content-Ad Singularity Loop**: publish high-volume organic content, then promote only proven winners.
2. **Intent Capture Loop**: run always-on Google Search and Shopping to capture demand at the moment of intent.

## Priority Outcomes (Next 90 Days)

- Increase qualified lead volume while maintaining efficiency.
- Lift conversion rate through funnel redesign and friction tuning.
- Improve budget efficiency through better targeting, negatives, and smart bidding stability.
- Establish reliable measurement for LTV and contribution margin decisions.

## Core Strategic Principles

- **Proof over promise**: trust declines as spend scales, so proof assets must increase.
- **Reskin winners**: most creative budget should iterate on proven concepts.
- **Search always-on**: Search is mandatory bottom-funnel capture, not optional testing.
- **Control through structure**: strict campaign architecture and execution cadence reduce waste.
- **Scale only with data readiness**: expansion channels activate after conversion stability is reached.

## Deliverables in This Pack

- Platform strategy for Google Ads and LinkedIn Ads.
- Creative testing and funnel optimization framework.
- Measurement and attribution operating model.
- 30-60-90 day implementation roadmap.
