# Ads Logic — AI-Native Google Ads (2026)

## Paradigm

- Search is intent-based, not keyword-based. Queries change daily (15%+); optimize for themes, not strings.
- AI Mode (Broad Match, PMax, AMAX) is the primary traffic layer. No adoption = no visibility.
- CTR is down (up to -61%), CPC is up. Optimize for value per click, not click volume.

---

## Campaign Architecture

| Campaign | Role | Funnel Stage |
|---|---|---|
| Search | Intent capture, always-on | Bottom |
| Shopping | Product-driven intent | Bottom |
| Performance Max | Discovery + expansion | Mid |
| Demand Gen | Awareness + remarketing | Top |

**Launch sequence:**
1. Search + Shopping (day 1)
2. Add PMax after 30 conversions / 30 days
3. Add Demand Gen at scale phase

Search is not an experiment — it captures the final ~30% of the journey and must run continuously.

---

## Keyword & Targeting

**Ad group structure:** 1 intent theme per group → 1–4 Broad Match long-tail keywords + Exact Match anchor.

**Broad Match** (primary driver): Use `[product + modifier + benefit]` format (e.g., "men's black gym shirts that are quick drying") to capture conversational queries and feed AI intent signals.

**Exact Match** (control layer): Use for B2B / high CPC niches, filtering irrelevant traffic, and early-stage campaigns.

**Conversational optimization:** Write ad copy that mirrors full-sentence queries. Use keyword insertion and adaptive headlines.

---

## Shopping & Feed

- Merchant Center controls visibility — keywords do not.
- Product titles (critical): First 50 characters = `[Intent] + [Modifier] + [Brand]`.
- Feed attributes are AI signals — include benefits, use cases, and functional keywords.
- Launch with Manual CPC or Max Clicks → switch to tROAS after stability.

---

## Performance Max

- Do not launch before 30 conversions / 30 days.
- Use Asset Groups (not ad groups). Keep separate from Search campaigns.
- Apply negative keywords and brand exclusions to prevent cannibalization.
- Lead gen: only use with offline conversion tracking (risk: low-quality leads).

---

## Demand Gen

- Platforms: YouTube, Display, Discovery.
- Use when Search CPC is too high, product needs education, or scaling beyond Meta.
- Budget requirement: daily ≥ 20× CPA.
- Separate ad groups by placement: Shorts, Instream, Display.
- Targeting: lookalikes + remarketing.

---

## Bidding & Budget

- Smart Bidding is mandatory (Maximize Conversions or tCPA/tROAS).
- Data requirement: 30 conversions / 30 days, <20% weekly variance.
- Daily budget ≈ CPC × 10 (target ≥10 clicks/day).
- 60-day stability rule — avoid frequent bid changes. Performance may drop before recovering.
- High tROAS/tCPA targets restrict growth; lower them to allow scaling.
- Ignore CPC fluctuations — focus on profit margin.

---

## Negative Keywords

Build exclusion themes around:
- **Informational:** jobs, salary, how-to
- **Navigational:** login, download, support
- **Irrelevant services:** free, repair, DIY

Weekly process: check top-spend queries → identify non-converting themes → add negatives. Use a 3–4 month data window; avoid over-pruning early.

---

## Conversion

- First 8 seconds determine success.
- Replace static landing pages with multi-step funnels.
- Apply value anchoring and commitment escalation.
- Increase value per user: upsells, email capture, flexible payment.
- Benchmark: average CVR ~2.3%. Target: 8–15%.

---

## Measurement

- Last-click attribution is invalid. Customers take 6–20 touchpoints.
- Core metrics: LTV (12+ months), Contribution Margin, ECNV (cost per new visit).
- Track invisible conversions: brand search lift, direct traffic growth.
- Technical setup: Enhanced Conversions + GTM + offline conversion tracking (lead gen).

---

## GEO (AI Visibility)

Optimize for Google AI Overviews. Build authority through clear positioning, consistent messaging, and strong content.

## STAB Framework

| Layer | Focus |
|---|---|
| **S**pending | Budget allocation per campaign |
| **T**argeting | Intent themes + Broad Match + negatives |
| **A**ds | Dynamic, intent-aligned copy + funnel landing |
| **B**idding | Smart bidding with data stability |

---

## Execution Order

1. Identify business type (Ecom / Lead Gen / B2B)
2. Launch Search + Shopping
3. Build intent-based keyword themes
4. Structure ad groups (Broad + Exact)
5. Apply negative keyword guardrails
6. Launch and collect data
7. Hit stability (30 conversions / 30 days)
8. Add PMax
9. Add Demand Gen
10. Weekly: follow spend → refine negatives
11. Optimize conversion funnel
12. Measure LTV + contribution margin
