# Google Ads Case Studies: Real-World Campaigns That Succeeded

A collection of real-world Google Ads campaigns with step-by-step breakdowns covering input, process, and output.

---

## 1. Asutra Self-Care Products

**Industry:** Wellness / E-commerce

### Input
- Zero website traffic starting in 2018
- Targeted wellness keywords like "topical magnesium"
- Budget allocated across Search, Shopping, and Performance Max campaigns

### Process
1. Launched broad awareness campaigns via Search and Shopping ads
2. Shifted strategy to Performance Max for AI-optimized multichannel reach
3. Iteratively refined ad creatives and bidding strategies
4. Focused optimization on ROAS improvement over time

### Output
- 300,000+ clicks generated
- Six-figure revenue achieved
- 2x+ ROAS with 84% lift attributed to Performance Max
- 75% revenue growth sustained over 3 years

## 3. Just Go Engagement Overhaul

**Industry:** Travel / Lifestyle

### Input
- Low click-through rate (CTR) on existing campaigns
- Broad audience data with no precise segmentation

### Process
1. Performed a comprehensive campaign audit
2. Applied precise demographic and interest-based targeting
3. A/B tested multiple ad copy variants and visual creatives
4. Identified and scaled the highest-performing combinations

### Output
- 921% increase in CTR

## 4. Books2Door E-commerce

**Industry:** E-commerce / Books

### Input
- Generic, broad-match keywords with weak purchase intent
- Suboptimal ad copy not aligned with buyer motivation

### Process
1. Refined keyword list to buyer-intent and transactional terms
2. A/B tested creative variations to identify top performers
3. Rebuilt landing pages with a conversion-first focus
4. Aligned ad messaging with landing page content for Quality Score improvement

### Output
- 33% uplift in conversion rate

## 5. Lawyer Lead Tracking Fix

**Industry:** Legal Services

### Input
- Two Search campaigns with mismatched conversion goals
- Service-specific tracking errors causing inaccurate data
- 13 conversions at $365 CPA (baseline)

### Process
1. Reconfigured conversion goals per individual campaign objective
2. Added phrase-match keywords to improve search relevance
3. Switched bidding strategy to Maximize Conversions
4. Monitored and validated tracking accuracy post-fix

### Output
- Conversions increased from 13 to 43
- CPA dropped from $365 to $110

---

## 6. G-Code Tactical Shopping Ads

**Industry:** Tactical Gear / E-commerce

### Input
- Low impression share in Shopping auctions
- Product feed data flags and disapprovals from Google

### Process
1. Diagnosed and fixed product feed issues using Google Merchant Center feedback
2. Improved ad relevance and Quality Scores across product listings
3. Enhanced landing page experience for better post-click engagement
4. Monitored auction insights to validate improvement

### Output
- Higher CTR and conversion volume
- Improved ROAS through better auction competitiveness

---

## 7. Low-Budget Course Sales

**Industry:** Online Education

### Input
- $255 total campaign budget
- Competitive niche with high keyword costs
- Single course product with a defined sales deadline

### Process
1. Wrote high-relevance ad copy tightly matched to the landing page content
2. Started with broad match keywords, then refined to phrase match for quality traffic
3. Focused spend on highest-intent search queries
4. Maintained message consistency from ad to landing page for high Quality Scores

### Output
- 12% average CTR (peaking at 28%)
- $1.37 average CPC
- 90% lead-to-sale conversion rate
- Course fully sold out within budget
