# LinkedIn Ads Case Studies: Real-World Campaigns That Succeeded

A collection of real-world LinkedIn Ads campaigns with step-by-step breakdowns covering input, process, and output.

---

## 1. HubSpot — B2B Lead Generation via Sponsored Content

**Industry:** Marketing / SaaS

### Input
- Target audience: marketing professionals at small-to-medium businesses
- Goal: generate high-quality leads for ebooks, webinars, and how-to guides
- Existing content assets ready for promotion
- Challenge: other platforms delivering low-quality leads with high noise

### Process
1. Launched LinkedIn Sponsored Content campaigns promoting top-performing content offers
2. Used LinkedIn's precise targeting to segment by company, job title, job function, and professional groups
3. Served ads natively in LinkedIn feeds across desktop, mobile, and tablet
4. Implemented LinkedIn Lead Gen Forms to pre-fill user data and reduce conversion friction
5. Continuously monitored CTR, CPL, and lead quality vs. other paid channels
6. Reallocated budget toward LinkedIn as performance data validated superiority over other networks

### Output
- 400% more leads within their target audience vs. other paid platforms
- CTR of 1–3%, which was 60% higher than other social networks
- Average CPC of ~$3 — significantly lower for the target professional segment
- Higher-quality leads that converted faster into customers

## 2. Salesforce — Dreamforce Event Campaign via Live Event Ads

**Industry:** CRM / Enterprise SaaS

### Input
- Annual Dreamforce conference requiring global audience reach beyond existing customer base
- Goal: drive registrations, live viewership, and pipeline growth
- Integrated Salesforce Data Cloud + Marketing Cloud with LinkedIn's ad platform
- Ad formats: Live Event Ads, Thought Leader Ads, Sponsored Content

### Process
1. Pre-event: Ran Sponsored Content and Live Event Ads targeting business decision-makers and AI enthusiasts to build awareness and drive RSVPs to the livestream
2. Used Salesforce Data Cloud to identify LinkedIn profiles with high probability of finding event content relevant to their role and industry
3. During event: Delivered real-time Live Event Ads pointing directly to the main keynote livestream
4. Post-event: Retargeted engaged viewers with Thought Leader Ads and session replay content to nurture pipeline
5. Measured cost-per-5-minute-viewer as the primary efficiency metric

### Output
- 3.4 million total views; 2.5 million scroll views
- 46,500+ viewers stayed engaged for 5+ minutes
- 30% lower cost-per-5-minute-viewer than anticipated
- LinkedIn campaign activations influenced over 53% of total Dreamforce pipeline
- Connection requests increased 24%; InMail acceptance rose 52% among participants
- 12% lift in Dreamforce registrations vs. the prior year

## 3. Adobe — Carousel Ads for Creative Cloud

**Industry:** Software / Creative Tools

### Input
- Goal: educate marketing professionals and decision-makers on Creative Cloud's features and benefits
- Target audience: digital marketing managers, CMOs, IT executives at mid-to-large companies
- Previous single-image ads underperforming on engagement and conversion

### Process
1. Replaced single-image ads with LinkedIn Carousel Ads for multi-card product storytelling
2. Each carousel card highlighted a distinct feature, benefit, or customer success story
3. Paired strong visuals with compelling headlines per card to encourage full swipe-through
4. Used Sponsored Content and Video Ads to drive traffic to the Experience Cloud product page
5. A/B tested carousel creative vs. original single-image format to quantify uplift
6. Mid-to-lower funnel: Retargeted website visitors with Event Ads for product demos

### Output
- 45% higher CTR vs. single-image ad campaigns
- 33% higher conversion rate vs. single-image campaigns
- 31% increase in attributed conversions (via Real-Time CDP integration for full buyer journey visibility)
- Increased inquiries and qualified leads for Creative Cloud and Experience Cloud

## 4. MobyCap — Full-Funnel B2B Finance Lead Generation

**Industry:** B2B Financial Services / Lending

### Input
- Rising CPCs in a competitive lending market
- Lead quality misaligned with sales process requirements
- Campaign structure lacking full-funnel coverage
- Goal: increase qualified lead volume while improving cost efficiency

### Process
1. Conducted comprehensive competitor analysis and market audit to identify structural gaps
2. Built a full-funnel campaign architecture:
   - TOFU: Awareness campaigns targeting cold ICP audiences (job titles, company size, industry)
   - MOFU: Retargeting campaigns for warm prospects who engaged with TOFU content
   - BOFU: LinkedIn Lead Gen Forms with custom qualifier questions to pre-screen intent
3. Integrated LinkedIn Lead Gen Forms with CRM to auto-route qualified prospects by sales stage
4. Built custom audiences from video views and website visitors — hit with highly relevant retargeting ads
5. Applied manual bidding strategy for tighter cost control vs. LinkedIn automated bidding
6. A/B tested single-image vs. video creatives across audience segments

### Output
- 1,733% increase in total conversions
- 800% increase in qualified leads
- 200%+ improvement in conversion rates for retargeted audiences vs. cold traffic
- Scaled LinkedIn Ads budget from $10K/month to $100K/month over 6 months

## 5. ESCP Europe — Global Student Recruitment via Spotlight Ads

**Industry:** Higher Education

### Input
- Target: prospective master's degree applicants globally
- Goal: 250 high-quality leads and 1 completed application per 100 leads
- Challenge: reaching highly specialized academic profiles across multiple countries

### Process
1. Launched a LinkedIn Sponsored Content campaign promoting scholarship information and program details
2. Used LinkedIn Spotlight Ads (Dynamic Ad format) to personalize ad content per viewer profile
3. Targeted by education level, field of study interest, career stage, and geography
4. Ads featured current students on campus to create aspirational, relatable imagery
5. Post-click: Nurtured leads through follow-up calls and structured interview invitation sequences
6. Monitored lead quality and conversion by country to identify highest-performing markets

### Output
- 2 million+ impressions among potential applicants
- 14% conversion rate — nearly double the original target
- 40 leads generated above the goal
- ~300 qualified applicants captured
- Identified 3 top-performing countries to anchor the global lead pipeline

## 6. Automation Anywhere — Product Launch via LinkedIn Live

**Industry:** Enterprise Software / RPA

### Input
- New product launch requiring authentic real-time engagement with existing community
- Goal: reach qualified technical and business decision-maker audiences
- Content: repurposed clips from previous videos combined with live product announcement

### Process
1. Planned and promoted a LinkedIn Live broadcast centered on the product launch announcement
2. Repurposed existing video content as pre-show clips to warm up the audience
3. Product marketers hosted the broadcast live, engaging directly with viewer comments in real time
4. Ran LinkedIn Sponsored Content and Message Ads to drive live viewership from target audiences
5. Post-broadcast: Used session replays as retargeting content for follow-up campaigns

### Output
- 400 comments within the first few minutes of going live
- 1,000 total comments by broadcast end
- 78% of all viewers came from LinkedIn Live (vs. all other platforms combined)
- Deep community engagement; meaningful product conversations with decision-makers at scale

## 7. Snagajob — Hourly Job Ads via LinkedIn Job & Video Ads

**Industry:** HR Tech / Job Marketplace

### Input
- Platform connecting job seekers to hourly work opportunities
- Goal: reach working professionals and re-engage them with new job opening alerts
- Challenge: hourly job content typically seen as low-relevance in professional LinkedIn context

### Process
1. Combined LinkedIn Job Ads with short Video Ads to add storytelling to job listings
2. Targeted by job title, industry, and skills — reaching professionals who matched open roles
3. Job Ads surfaced open positions to users outside existing follower networks
4. Video Ads told the story of the platform's value proposition for both employers and job seekers
5. Monitored cost-per-lead and lead conversion rate as primary optimization signals
6. Shifted budget to top-performing audience segments based on conversion data

### Output
- 84% rise in converted leads
- Higher applicant volume with improved lead quality
- Lower cost per lead vs. prior campaign benchmarks
- Validated LinkedIn as a scalable channel for hourly job discovery
