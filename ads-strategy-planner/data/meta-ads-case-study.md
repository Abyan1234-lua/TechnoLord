# Meta Ads Case Studies: Real-World Campaigns That Succeeded

A collection of real-world Meta Ads campaigns with step-by-step breakdowns covering input, process, and output.

---

## 1. Cathay United Bank — Credit Card Acquisitions

**Industry:** Financial Services / Banking

### Input
- Low credit card application volume
- Existing product catalog for credit card offerings
- Budget allocated to Advantage+ Shopping campaigns

### Process
1. Uploaded and optimized catalog sets within Meta's product feed
2. Enabled automated placements across Facebook and Instagram
3. Let Meta's Advantage+ bidding system identify high-intent users automatically
4. Monitored performance signals to validate automation decisions

### Output
- More credit card applications collected
- Lower cost-per-acquisition (CPA) compared to manual campaign setup

---

## 2. Castlery Furniture — Catalog Efficiency

**Industry:** Home Furnishings / E-commerce

### Input
- Full furniture inventory catalog
- Video creative assets per product category
- Budget allocated to Advantage+ Catalog campaigns

### Process
1. Activated dynamic product ads with video format for higher engagement
2. Delegated targeting, bidding, and placement decisions to Meta AI
3. Tested and iterated creatives based on engagement and conversion signals
4. Allowed automation to allocate spend toward best-performing combinations

### Output
- Higher ROAS and increased sales volume
- Improved efficiency through full-funnel automation

---

## 3. Gameduo — Gaming ROAS Scaling

**Industry:** Mobile Gaming

### Input
- Historical user purchase data
- Identified high-value customer segments within existing user base

### Process
1. Built value-based lookalike audiences from top-spending users
2. Layered Meta app campaigns for broader scaling with retained precision
3. Applied performance rules to automatically cut underperforming ad sets
4. Refined audience expansion iteratively based on ROAS thresholds

### Output
- Significant ROAS increase through precise audience expansion
- Scaled reach without sacrificing conversion quality

---

## 4. AppSumo — $2M Budget Experiment

**Industry:** SaaS / Digital Marketplace

### Input
- $2,000,000 test budget for sales and email list growth
- Initial narrow audience segments as starting baselines

### Process
1. Launched iterative small-budget tests across multiple ad variations
2. Identified winning creatives and audiences using real-time performance data
3. Rapidly scaled budget to top performers
4. Cut underperforming campaigns immediately based on predefined metrics

### Output
- $267,000 net profit after ad spend and platform fees
- Validated a repeatable test-and-scale framework at high budget levels

---

## 5. Aurum Brothers — Luxury Watch Retargeting

**Industry:** Luxury Goods / E-commerce

### Input
- Warm retargeting audiences: cart abandoners, product viewers, site visitors
- Full watch catalog for dynamic ad rendering

### Process
1. Built segmented retargeting audiences by funnel stage (view → cart → checkout)
2. Deployed carousel ads for dynamic product display personalized per user
3. Optimized campaigns for purchase conversions across all placements
4. Applied budget weighting toward highest-intent audience segments

### Output
- Monthly sales doubled (2x growth)
- 50% of total revenue attributed directly to Meta Ads
- 13x ROAS achieved

---

## 6. Seltzer Goods — Revenue Surge

**Industry:** Consumer Goods / E-commerce

### Input
- Broad product lineup with multiple SKUs
- Large untapped cold audiences during COVID demand surge
- Existing retargeting lists from prior site traffic

### Process
1. Scaled ad spend aggressively with a mix of video and static image creatives
2. Combined broad cold audience targeting with warm retargeting layers
3. Aligned ad messaging with optimized, conversion-focused landing pages
4. Continuously tested creative formats to sustain performance at scale

### Output
- 785% monthly revenue growth
- 9.68x ROAS

---

## 7. Kirnani Technologies — SaaS Lead Generation

**Industry:** B2B SaaS / CRM

### Input
- ₹30,000 (~$360) campaign budget
- Target audience: SaaS and CRM decision-makers, ages 25–45, in Tier 1 and Tier 2 Indian cities

### Process
1. Built lookalike and custom audiences from existing CRM and website data
2. Deployed native Meta Lead Forms to reduce friction in the signup flow
3. Applied behavioral and interest-based targeting layered over demographic filters
4. Optimized campaign delivery for lead quality over raw lead volume

### Output
- 300+ high-quality leads generated within 14 days
- Strong cost-per-lead efficiency on a limited budget
