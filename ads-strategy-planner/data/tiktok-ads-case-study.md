# TikTok Ads Case Studies: Real-World Campaigns That Succeeded

A collection of real-world TikTok Ads campaigns with step-by-step breakdowns covering input, process, and output.

---

## 1. Gymshark — #Gymshark66 Challenge

**Industry:** Fitness Apparel / E-commerce

### Input
- Established fitness influencer network on Instagram, seeking TikTok expansion
- New platform audience: Gen Z, humor-driven, trend-sensitive
- Goal: brand awareness + community habit-building around fitness
- Prize incentive: one year's free supply of Gymshark products

### Process
1. Launched the #Gymshark66 Challenge — users commit to a 66-day fitness goal and document their before/after journey
2. Partnered with 6 TikTok fitness and lifestyle creators (dance, acrobatics, fitness twins) to seed the challenge
3. Creators posted choreographed, trend-native content using branded hashtag and @gymshark tag
4. Amplified top-performing creator posts via TikTok Spark Ads
5. Encouraged UGC by making participation the entry mechanic for the prize draw

### Output
- 45.5 million views on #gymshark66
- 1.9 million likes and 12,576 comments
- 134,600+ TikTok videos created by users (as of 2026)
- 65.7 million total engagements across the campaign lifecycle

## 2. Wet n Wild — Big Poppa Mascara Launch

**Industry:** Beauty / Cosmetics

### Input
- New mascara product (Big Poppa) with zero existing awareness
- Goal: mass brand awareness among TikTok's young beauty audience
- Budget for a full-platform blitz: paid ads, creators, custom audio

### Process
1. Commissioned TikTok artist JVKE to create a custom branded sound for the campaign
2. Launched a Branded Hashtag Challenge to drive UGC participation
3. Deployed In-Feed Ads alongside the challenge to extend paid reach
4. Recruited TikTok creators to seed authentic product-use content
5. Used Spark Ads to amplify top-performing creator videos organically

### Output
- Massive brand awareness lift during product launch window
- Strong UGC volume driven by the hashtag challenge mechanic
- Established Big Poppa as a known product within TikTok's beauty community

## 3. MYKA Jewelry (via Tenengroup) — Father's Day Campaign

**Industry:** Jewelry / E-commerce

### Input
- Handmade personalized jewelry brand targeting gift buyers for Father's Day
- Key objective: lower cost-per-action (CPA) while increasing conversions
- Existing customer purchase data available for audience exclusion

### Process
1. Created TikTok-native product videos optimized for the For You page algorithm
2. Deployed TikTok Ads targeting relevant gift-buyer audiences
3. Excluded recent purchasers (last 14 days) from paid targeting to avoid wasted spend
4. Built retargeting campaigns for users who watched 6+ seconds of video or took website actions
5. Iterated creative based on real-time performance signals

### Output
- 53% decrease in cost-per-action (CPA)
- 52% increase in conversion rate

## 4. Garnier — #GlowUpChallenge

**Industry:** Skincare / FMCG

### Input
- Facial skincare product line needing awareness lift among younger consumers
- Hashtag Challenge Plus format selected for shoppable integration
- Goal: brand awareness + direct product sell-through via TikTok

### Process
1. Launched #GlowUpChallenge using TikTok's Hashtag Challenge Plus feature
2. Challenge Plus enabled in-app product browsing directly from the hashtag page
3. Seeded challenge with beauty creators to drive initial participation
4. Connected ad placements to TikTok Shop for frictionless purchase flow
5. Monitored UGC volume and engagement to gauge campaign momentum

### Output
- 2 million+ views on the hashtag challenge
- 2,000+ units of Garnier facial products sold directly through the campaign

## 5. Momentary Ink — Temporary Tattoo Product Demo

**Industry:** Beauty / Novelty Products / E-commerce

### Input
- Temporary tattoo brand with a visually demonstrable product
- Goal: product awareness and direct conversions via TikTok ads
- Creative approach: authentic product-use demonstration videos

### Process
1. Produced short-form demo videos showing the tattoo application process end-to-end
2. Leaned into authenticity — real transformations over polished production
3. Used emotional music and reveal pacing to build viewer retention
4. Ran In-Feed Ads with a direct purchase CTA linked to product page
5. Optimized for conversions, targeting users interested in beauty and self-expression

### Output
- 14 million views on campaign content
- 22% better cost-per-action (CPA) than industry benchmark

## 6. Slate & Tell — Personalized Jewelry Showcase

**Industry:** Jewelry / E-commerce

### Input
- Personalized jewelry brand with a unique customization process
- Product differentiation: the *process* of making jewelry is as sellable as the product
- Goal: reach new audiences and drive add-to-cart behavior

### Process
1. Filmed behind-the-scenes content showing the jewelry customization process
2. Used process-reveal storytelling format native to TikTok's content style
3. Ran In-Feed Ads targeting users interested in gifts, jewelry, and personalization
4. Linked ads directly to product pages with customization options
5. Measured success via reach and add-to-cart sessions as primary KPIs

### Output
- 4 million users reached
- 1,000 add-to-cart sessions generated
- 2x return on ad spend (ROAS)

## 7. Sony Music — #Atrevete Challenge (Spain / Latin Markets)

**Industry:** Music / Entertainment

### Input
- Sony Music needed to promote a new track across Spain, Argentina, and Italy
- Goal: drive streams and cultural adoption through localized TikTok content
- Budget for influencer seeding and paid amplification

### Process
1. Identified 12 key TikTok influencers with strong reach in target markets
2. Briefed influencers to launch the #Atrevete challenge with localized, culturally relevant content
3. Challenge mechanic encouraged user participation through dance and creative response videos
4. Spark Ads amplified top-performing influencer posts to broaden reach beyond organic
5. Monitored UGC volume and hashtag traction as leading performance indicators

### Output
- 19.9 million users reached
- 10,300 user-generated videos created
- 8 million engaged users
- 1.2 million likes on the hashtag challenge
