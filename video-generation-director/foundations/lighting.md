# lighting

## natural_light

- **Description:** Sunlight or ambient light
- **Use_Case:** UGC, lifestyle
- **Effect:** Real, authentic

## soft_light

- **Description:** Diffused light, low shadows
- **Use_Case:** Beauty, interviews
- **Effect:** Flattering, smooth

## hard_light

- **Description:** Strong directional shadows
- **Use_Case:** Dramatic ads
- **Effect:** High contrast, bold

## backlighting

- **Description:** Light behind subject
- **Use_Case:** Silhouettes, cinematic shots
- **Effect:** Dramatic, separation

## rim_light

- **Description:** Light outlining subject edges
- **Use_Case:** Product, portraits
- **Effect:** Depth, subject separation

## high_key

- **Description:** Bright, evenly lit
- **Use_Case:** Commercial, upbeat ads
- **Effect:** Clean, positive

## low_key

- **Description:** Dark, shadow-heavy
- **Use_Case:** Luxury, cinematic ads
- **Effect:** Dramatic, moody

## practical_lighting

- **Description:** Light sources in scene (lamp, screen)
- **Use_Case:** Realistic environments
- **Effect:** Immersive

## colored_lighting

- **Description:** Use of colored lights
- **Use_Case:** Tech, music, fashion
- **Effect:** Stylized, modern

## spotlight

- **Description:** Focused beam on subject
- **Use_Case:** Highlight product
- **Effect:** Attention isolation
