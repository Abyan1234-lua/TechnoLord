# camera_movement

## static

- **Description:** Camera does not move
- **Use_Case:** Product focus, interviews
- **Effect:** Clean, professional

## pan

- **Description:** Horizontal rotation
- **Use_Case:** Reveal scene or subject
- **Effect:** Observational

## tilt

- **Description:** Vertical movement
- **Use_Case:** Reveal height or scale
- **Effect:** Dramatic reveal

## dolly_in

- **Description:** Camera moves forward
- **Use_Case:** Emphasize subject
- **Effect:** Builds intensity

## dolly_out

- **Description:** Camera moves backward
- **Use_Case:** Reveal context or isolation
- **Effect:** Emotional distancing

## tracking

- **Description:** Camera follows subject
- **Use_Case:** Movement, lifestyle shots
- **Effect:** Immersive

## handheld

- **Description:** Natural handheld motion
- **Use_Case:** UGC, raw ads
- **Effect:** Authentic, real

## steadicam

- **Description:** Stabilized smooth movement
- **Use_Case:** Cinematic ads
- **Effect:** Professional, fluid

## crane_jib

- **Description:** Vertical sweeping movement
- **Use_Case:** Big reveals
- **Effect:** Cinematic scale

## zoom_in

- **Description:** Lens zooms in
- **Use_Case:** Focus shift
- **Effect:** Urgency or attention

## zoom_out

- **Description:** Lens zooms out
- **Use_Case:** Reveal context
- **Effect:** Perspective shift

## whip_pan

- **Description:** Fast pan blur transition
- **Use_Case:** Transitions, fast ads
- **Effect:** High energy
