# shot_types

## extreme_close_up

- **Purpose:** Show micro detail (eyes, texture, product surface)
- **Use_Case:** Beauty, food, luxury, emotional intensity
- **Effect:** Highly intimate, sensory trigger
- **Notes:** Use shallow depth of field

## close_up

- **Purpose:** Focus on face or key subject
- **Use_Case:** Emotion, testimonials, product highlights
- **Effect:** Personal, engaging

## medium_close_up

- **Purpose:** Head and shoulders framing
- **Use_Case:** Dialogue, talking head ads
- **Effect:** Balanced intimacy and context

## medium_shot

- **Purpose:** Show subject waist-up with context
- **Use_Case:** Lifestyle ads, demonstrations
- **Effect:** Natural storytelling

## wide_shot

- **Purpose:** Show full subject and environment
- **Use_Case:** Establishing, branding
- **Effect:** Cinematic, spatial awareness

## extreme_wide_shot

- **Purpose:** Show vast environment
- **Use_Case:** Luxury, travel, cinematic ads
- **Effect:** Epic scale, emotional distance

## over_the_shoulder

- **Purpose:** Frame subject from behind another
- **Use_Case:** Conversations, tutorials
- **Effect:** Immersive, narrative-driven

## pov_shot

- **Purpose:** First-person perspective
- **Use_Case:** UGC ads, TikTok style
- **Effect:** Authentic, relatable

## tracking_subject

- **Purpose:** Follow subject movement
- **Use_Case:** Action, product-in-use
- **Effect:** Dynamic, engaging

## static_shot

- **Purpose:** No camera movement
- **Use_Case:** Product hero, interviews
- **Effect:** Stable, controlled
