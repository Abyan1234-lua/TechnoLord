# hook_patterns

## pattern_interrupt

- **Description:** Break viewer expectation instantly
- **Shots:**
  - Sudden motion / unexpected visual
  - Smash cut
  - Bold text overlay
- **Use_Case:** TikTok, Reels, short ads
- **Effect:** Scroll stopping
- **Notes:** First 12 seconds critical

## direct_address

- **Description:** Subject talks directly to camera
- **Shots:**
  - Close-up / medium close-up
  - Eye contact
- **Use_Case:** UGC, testimonials
- **Effect:** Personal, trustworthy

## curiosity_gap

- **Description:** Show something incomplete or strange
- **Shots:**
  - Partial reveal
  - Slow zoom or static tension
- **Use_Case:** Premium, storytelling ads
- **Effect:** Viewer wants answer

## problem_opening

- **Description:** Start with pain point
- **Shots:**
  - Frustration close-up
  - Situation breakdown
- **Use_Case:** SaaS, product ads
- **Effect:** Immediate relatability

## bold_claim

- **Description:** Strong statement or promise
- **Shots:**
  - Text-first or voiceover
  - Supporting visual
- **Use_Case:** Direct response ads
- **Effect:** High attention + skepticism trigger

## before_after_hook

- **Description:** Show transformation instantly
- **Shots:**
  - Split screen or fast cut
- **Use_Case:** Fitness, beauty
- **Effect:** Clear value instantly

## visual_shock

- **Description:** Surprising or extreme visual
- **Shots:**
  - Unexpected scenario
  - High contrast imagery
- **Use_Case:** Viral ads
- **Effect:** Pattern break + curiosity
