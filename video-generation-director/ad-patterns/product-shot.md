# product_shots

## hero_shot

- **Description:** Main product focus
- **Shots:**
  - Centered composition
  - Clean background
  - Slow dolly or static
- **Lighting:** Controlled / studio
- **Effect:** Brand identity anchor

## in_use

- **Description:** Product in real context
- **Shots:**
  - Medium or tracking shot
  - Natural interaction
- **Effect:** Relatability, usability

## problem_solution_pair

- **Description:** Show problem then product solving it
- **Shots:**
  - Problem close-up
  - Cut to product usage
- **Effect:** Logical persuasion

## macro_detail

- **Description:** Highlight texture/features
- **Shots:**
  - Extreme close-up
  - Slow motion
- **Effect:** Sensory appeal, premium feel

## comparison

- **Description:** Product vs alternative
- **Shots:**
  - Split frame or sequence
- **Effect:** Clear differentiation

## transformation

- **Description:** Result after using product
- **Shots:**
  - Before → after sequence
- **Effect:** Outcome-driven persuasion

## exploded_view

- **Description:** Show internal parts / layers
- **Shots:**
  - Animated breakdown
- **Effect:** Transparency, trust

## ugc_product

- **Description:** Casual product showcase
- **Shots:**
  - Handheld POV
  - Imperfect framing
- **Effect:** Authenticity
