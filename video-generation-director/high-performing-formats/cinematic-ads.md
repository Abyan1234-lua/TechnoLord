# cinematic_ads

## core_principle

Emotion over information, feeling over explanation

## pacing

Slow to moderate (intentional timing)

## structure

### opening

- Wide or atmospheric shot
- Set tone and mood

### build

- Visual storytelling
- Minimal dialogue

### peak

- Emotional or visual climax

### resolution

- Brand or product reveal

## shot_style

- Wide cinematic shots
- Macro details
- Slow motion
- Smooth camera movement

## lighting

- Controlled, stylized
- High contrast or soft luxury

## editing_rules

- Longer shot duration
- Music-driven pacing
- Fewer cuts, more intention

## psychology

- Emotional resonance
- Identity and aspiration
- Brand perception

## fail_patterns

- Too abstract (no message)
- No brand connection
- Overly slow pacing without payoff
