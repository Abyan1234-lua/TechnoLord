# tiktok_ads

## core_principle

Capture attention instantly, maintain rapid engagement, deliver value fast

## pacing

Very fast (cut every 1–2 seconds)

## structure

### 0-2s

**Hook:**

- POV or direct address
- Pattern interrupt
- Bold or curiosity statement

### 2-6s

**Engagement:**

- Problem or intrigue
- Quick cuts (3–5 shots)

### 6-12s

**Value:**

- Product demo or payoff
- Show benefit clearly

### 12-20s

**Retention Loop:**

- Add new angle or twist
- Prevent drop-off

### final

**CTA:**

- Direct or implied

## shot_style

- POV shots
- Handheld camera
- Fast jump cuts
- Reaction shots

## editing_rules

- No slow intro
- Cut dead air
- Use captions always
- Visual change every ~2 seconds

## psychology

- Curiosity gap
- Relatability
- Instant gratification

## fail_patterns

- Slow cinematic opening
- Over-polished look
- No clear hook
