# youtube_ads

## core_principle

Hook before skip, then sustain attention with narrative or value

## pacing

Moderate (varies between fast and slow)

## structure

### 0-5s

**Hook:**

- Strong visual or statement
- Must prevent skip

### 5-15s

**Context:**

- Introduce problem or idea

### 15-45s

**Development:**

- Story or product explanation
- Combine multiple shot types

### 45-60s

**Payoff:**

- Results or key message

### end

**CTA:**

- Clear action

## shot_style

- Mix of cinematic + direct address
- B-roll overlays
- Product demonstrations

## editing_rules

- Pattern interrupts every 5–10 seconds
- Use music progression
- Combine storytelling + visuals

## psychology

- Narrative engagement
- Value building
- Trust over time

## fail_patterns

- Weak first 5 seconds
- Too long without variation
- No payoff
