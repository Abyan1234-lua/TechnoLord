## Template 1: New SEO Initiative (1-pager)

**Best for:** Pitching a specific SEO project to a CMO or CEO. Clean, fast to read.

---

**[Initiative Name]: SEO Investment Proposal**

**The opportunity**
There are an estimated [X] monthly searches for [problem/topic directly relevant to product]. We currently capture approximately [Y]% of that traffic. Our closest competitor, [Competitor], receives an estimated [Z] monthly organic visits in this category. Closing [%] of that gap translates to approximately [$] in additional pipeline at our current conversion rate.

**What we're building**
[One sentence: specific thing being built — e.g., "A programmatic landing page system covering 200 city-level pages for our core service offering."]

This addresses a real user need: [one sentence on the user problem being solved].

**The ask**
- Engineering: [X weeks / not required]
- Content: [$X or X hours]
- Timeline: [Start date → live date]
- Total investment: [$X or X person-weeks]

**Expected return**
- [X] additional monthly organic visits within [Y] months
- Estimated [$] in organic-influenced pipeline within [Z] months
- Payback period: [X months] at current conversion rate

**How we'll measure it**
Primary metric: [organic sessions / leads / revenue from organic]
90-day checkpoint: [specific milestone — e.g., "50 pages indexed, first 500 monthly visits"]

**What happens if we don't**
[Competitor] continues to widen their organic advantage. Our paid CAC continues to rise with no durable channel as a hedge. Catching up in 12 months costs [X% more / is [X] months further away].

## Template 3: Quick Slack/Email Ask

**Best for:** Informal request to a product lead or engineering manager for a small, specific item.

---

Hey [Name],

Quick ask: I've identified [specific SEO issue or opportunity] that's affecting [X pages / traffic]. The fix is [specific, scoped ask — e.g., "adding a canonical tag to 40 product pages" / "a 2-hour engineering change to our URL structure"].

Why it matters: [one concrete business impact — e.g., "These pages are currently not indexed by Google, costing us an estimated X monthly visits."]

The work: [X hours / already written up as a ticket here: [link]]. Doesn't require a design review.

Happy to walk through the data if useful. Would [date] work for a quick 15 minutes?

[Name]

## Template 4: Pilot Proposal

**Best for:** When the stakeholder is skeptical and needs to see proof before committing.

---

**SEO Pilot Proposal: [Initiative Name]**

**Objective:** Demonstrate that [specific SEO approach] generates [measurable outcome] within [90 days].

**Scope:** [Narrow, bounded version of the initiative — e.g., "Build 10 city landing pages, not the full 200"]

**Investment:**
- Engineering: [X days]
- Content: [X pieces / $X]
- Total: [$X / X person-days]

**Success criteria:**
By [date], we will have achieved:
- [ ] [X pages indexed]
- [ ] [Y monthly organic visits to pilot pages]
- [ ] [Z leads/conversions from organic]

**Go/no-go decision:** On [date], we review results against success criteria. If met, we proceed with full initiative. If not met, we document learnings and stop.

**Downside:** [Maximum cost if the pilot shows no signal] and clear learnings on why.
